Long-Term Complications

Uncontrolled diabetes can lead to complications like heart disease, kidney damage, and vision problems. Proper management reduces these risks.

Healthy Lifestyle Management

Combining diet, exercise, and medication is key to managing diabetes and leading a healthy, fulfilling life.

Mental Health & Diabetes

Living with diabetes can affect mental well-being. Managing stress and seeking support are important aspects of diabetes care.

Symptoms of Diabetes
  • Increased thirst and frequent urination
  • Extreme fatigue and weakness
  • Blurred vision
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Slow-healing sores or frequent infections

Each model offers different advantages. SVM is highly accurate for complex datasets, Logistic Regression is easy to interpret, Random Forest provides robust predictions, and KNN is simple and effective for smaller datasets. You can experiment with different models to see which works best for your data.

The accuracy of predictions depends on the quality of the input data and the model used. Generally, all models used in this system provide high accuracy, but they are not a substitute for medical advice and should be used as part of a broader diagnosis process.
